### Conclusion

**18:59** · So the world of Dark Souls 1 is pretty special.

**19:06** · Non-linearity lets you create your own adventure, and is perfect for second playthroughs and speedruns.

**19:12** · The interconnected pathways encourage you to memorise the geography and architecture of the world.
