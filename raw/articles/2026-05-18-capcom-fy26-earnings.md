# FY26/3 Earnings Summary, FY27/3 Plans, Mid- to Long-Term Growth Strategies
Capcom Co., Ltd. (TSE Prime, 9697)

## Major Takeaways (FY26/3)
- 13 consecutive years of OP growth, 11 consecutive years of over 10% OP growth
- Highest consolidated sales and operating profit in Capcom history
- Increased year-end dividend to ¥25, full-year dividend totals ¥45, dividend payout ratio 34.5%
- FY27/3 plan continues over 10% OP growth target; full-year dividend ¥46

## Mid-to-Long Term Strategy

### Management Vision
"A company that captivates people around the world with our best-in-class immersive content"

### Growth Outlook
- Sales Strategy: Grow Consumer titles sales through marketing initiatives
- IP Strategy: Expand fan base with multi-faceted IP use
- 10% annual OP growth target

### Market Overview (Newzoo Feb 2026)
- Mobile: 2.985B players
- Console + PC target: 1.5B (Console 645M, PC 935M)
- Global game player population: 3.5B
- Market value: $196B (2025) → $227B (2028E), ~¥36 trillion at 159 JPY/USD

### Flywheel Business Model
- Consumer (CS) New + Catalog → Character·Media·eSports·Mobile → Arcade Operations + Amusement Equipments → back to CS growth
- "Single Content Multiple Usage"

### Sales Strategy — Digital Sales Growth
- Catalog ratio trajectory: 18/3 45.1% → 26/3 83.7% → 27/3 plan 81.5%
- Digital unit sales trajectory: 18/3 1,290 → 26/3 5,493 → 27/3 plan 6,200 (10K units)

### IP Strategy — Multiple Usage
- Media, eSports, Game Licensing, Amusement Equipments, Arcade Operations → Increased Awareness & Brand Value → Global User Base Growth → back to popular game content (IP)
- Non-Consumer business growth contributes to game unit sales

### Brand Nurturing (next growth engines)
- Leading: Resident Evil, Monster Hunter, Street Fighter
- Nurturing: Okami, Dragon's Dogma, Ace Attorney, Onimusha, Mega Man, Devil May Cry, Dead Rising
- Methods: New IP, sequels, remakes, ports

### Development Capabilities
- Developers (non-consolidated): 14/3 1,368 → 26/3 2,842 (over 2× in 12 years)
- Cross-generational teams enable tech transfer (20s, 30s, 40s, 50s balance)
- R&D Building #3 adjacent to Osaka HQ — completion scheduled 2027
- Proprietary game development engine (RE Engine)

### Generative AI Use
- Decrease routine task time: research, draft generation, user analysis, interactive manuals, error checks, meeting notes
- Increase time for creative work
