# Benchmarks: How Many Wishlists Can I Get From Steam Next Fest?
Source: https://howtomarketagame.com/2025/03/26/benchmarks-how-many-wishlists-can-i-get-from-steam-next-fest/
Date: 2025-03

## Survey Base

182 survey responses from games in the February 2026 Steam Next Fest.

## Performance Tiers

| Tier | Wishlists Earned |
|------|-----------------|
| Diamond | 10,000+ |
| Gold | 7,000–9,999 |
| Silver | 1,000–6,999 |
| Bronze | 0–999 |

## Pre-Festival Momentum: The Strongest Predictor

Strongest correlation with wishlists earned = total wishlists before the festival (r = 0.825).

| Pre-Festival Wishlists | Median Wishlists Earned |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| 0–999 | 322 |
| 1,000–9,999 | 1,006 |
| 10,000–99,999 | 5,215 |
| 100,000+ | 12,882 |

## Demo Conversion Rate

Demo conversion rate = (players who played AND wishlisted) ÷ total players. Moderate correlation (r = 0.457). Optimal range: ~20%. Below average suggests presentation mismatch, not necessarily bad game.

## Algorithm Visibility Pattern

Steam provides baseline visibility initially, then rapidly concentrates impressions on top performers by day 3. "You are either a Valve favorite and they give you all the visibility or you are nothing to them."

## Strategic Recommendations

1. Launch demos well before the festival for iterative improvements
2. Avoid shadow-dropping demos immediately before SNF
3. Use separate demo store pages rather than main-page versions
4. Aim for 2,000+ pre-festival wishlists for optimal performance variance
5. Focus on demo quality — negative feedback often indicates presentation mismatches

Underlying game quality drives all metrics. Wishlists and velocity are indicators, not independent success factors.
